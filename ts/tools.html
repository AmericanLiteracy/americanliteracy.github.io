SoundSpel Translation Tools

Written as it Sounds. Pronounced as it's Written


Interactive translator: Give SoundSpel a try!

Click the green run button just below to start translation tool. Then type or paste your text, which appears in white font. You must press enter afterward, and translated text will appear in colors. On a windows system, right click on a mouse (double touch on a pad) and choose paste.
